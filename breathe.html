The 54321 method

Take your time and breathe deeply as you slowly go through this anxiety exercise


1. Focus On 5 Things You Can See

2. Focus On 4 Things You Can Touch

3. Focus On 3 Things You Can Hear

4. Focus On 2 Things You Can Smell

5. Focus On 1 Thing You Can Taste

Breathing exercise

Try this for a few minutes to calm down

Go outside or find a quiet place for this activity

Change the seconds to whatever suits you


1. Breathe in for 4 seconds

2. Hold your breath for 7 seconds

3. Breathe out for 8 seconds

Mindfullness activites

Try out some of these activities

- Go outside, listen to the birds and look at the nature

- Go on a walk or a run

- Try painting, crocheting, doodling or colouring

- Listen to music or play an instrument

- Start a journal

- Do some gardening or cooking

- Find a good book to read
